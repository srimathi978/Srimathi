# Espresso Martini

## Ingredients

- 50ml vodka
- 30ml Kahlúa
- 10ml sugar syrup
- 1 strong espresso
- 3 coffee beans, for garnish

## Method

1. Pour ingredients into a shaker filled with ice, shake vigorously, and strain into a chilled martini glass.
